Cell Game

Welcome to the Cell Game! In this game you will siliate the various functions that organelles inside of a cell perform. The purpose of the game is to produce proteins! In order to do so, there are various resources you must manage and actions you must take.

Resources

There are several resources which you must manage to be successful.
  • ATP ATP is a molelie that cells use for energy. Most actions require ATP to function.
  • Glucose: Glucose is a sugar molelie. Glucose is used to produce more ATP.
  • Oxygen: Oxygen is a gas necessary for life. Oxygen is used to produce more ATP.
  • Carbon Dioxide: Carbon dioxide is a waste produce produced after a cell performs respiration to produce more ATP. It has no use for the cell and must be released before it gets too high.
  • MET: MET is an amino acid. It is used to produce proteins.
  • SER: SER is an amino acid. It is used to produce proteins.
  • VAL VAL is an amino acid. It is used to produce proteins.
  • Protein Protein is an essential molelie for life. Proteins perform many different tasks. Cells produce proteins in the ribosome.

Organelles

Organelles are small structures found inside a cell. Each structure has a specific function.

Mitochondria

The mitochondria is responsible for producing ATP for the cell.
  • Produce ATP: Requires 1 glucose and 1 oxygen. Produces 3 ATP.

Cell Membrane

The cell membrane is responsible for protecting the cell and allowing materials in and out.
  • Get Glucose: Requires 1 ATP. Adds 1 glucose.
  • Get Amino Acid: Requires 1 ATP. Adds 1 MET, SER or VAL.
  • Get Oxygen: Adds 1 oxygen.
  • Release Carbon Dioxide: Releases all carbon dioxide in the cell.

Nucleus

The nucleus controls all cell activities. It also contains all DNA.
  • Check Gene: Requires 1 ATP. Checks a gene in the nucleus and provides list of necessary amino acids.

Ribosome

The ribosomes are where protein is synthesized.
  • Produce Protein: Requires 1 ATP. Produces a protein once the correct order of amino acids have been entered.
